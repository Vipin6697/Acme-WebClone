    By subscribing you are authorising recurring payments made to Microsoft at the intervals you selected, until cancelled or recurring billing is turned off. To stop being charged, cancel your subscription or turn off recurring billing in your Microsoft account (account.microsoft.com/services) or on your Xbox console, before the next billing date. Charges may be increased with at least 30 days notice as described in the Microsoft Store Terms of Sale. You can receive a refund within 30 days from your initial purchase by cancelling your subscription. This does not affect your additional rights under applicable law.
